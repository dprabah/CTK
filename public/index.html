Vision

To preserve, promote, and celebrate Tamil culture, heritage, and values while fostering a strong sense of unity and community among Tamil residents and immigrants in Germany.

Mission

To celebrate Tamil festivals and traditions, empower youth, support community integration, advocate for Tamil interests, and nurture cultural preservation and promotion.

Membership

Membership is open to all who share an interest in Tamil culture and heritage, irrespective of background, to join our vibrant community in Germany
